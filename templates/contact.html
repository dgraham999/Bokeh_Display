Dennis Graham

I am the founder of Brightfield Analytics. I have invested over 35 years in business startups, acquisitions, divestitures and early stage company development. That work required many business plans, budgets, valuations, cash flows, debt sources and equity placements.

The problem was always the sales forecast. A few years ago, I began studying machine learning and deep learning to solve this recurring problem. Eventually, I began applying deep learning solutions and found that the results were not just about accurate predictions but accurate customer information to build marketing and loyalty programs also.

I invite you to contact us so that we may share our results in greater detail with you.
